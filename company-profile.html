GUNNER Security

About Us

Gunner Security specialise in the provision of manned guarding services to clients in both public and private sectors including commercial establishments, corporate offices, retail & shopping centres, real estate & infrastructure projects, and residential premises.

The Company is based in Mumbai thus providing services primarily in MMRDA region; we can run healthier and cost effective operations and our experts and management available to reduce time to any location under MMRDA region.

We are a PSARA 2005 approved agency for the provision of security guarding and door supervision services across Maharashtra state. All our guards are underpinned by rigorous screening quality and vetting process of Service standards of PSARA Act 2005 and all guards are trained complying with the standards of PSARA Act 2005.

All clients' assignment is reviewed periodically by our experienced managers, service delivery and on-site performance is assessed and clients are encouraged to complete satisfaction surveys and provide regular feedback

At Gunner we understand that when considering your security requirements it is not only quality and reliability of services you receive that is important, but also the cost of those services. Our services rates are therefore are very competitive and will always reflect site surveys and risk assessments to ensure they are closely matched to your requirements as possible.
